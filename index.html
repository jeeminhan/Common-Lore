Common Lore

A conversational card game that weaves individual stories into a Shared Lore — connecting careers, cultures, homes, and wonders across borders.

1
Deal

Shuffle and deal 5 cards to each player. Remaining deck face-down in center.

2
Share

Clockwise, each player answers a card from their hand, then discards it.

3
Bridge Pass

Say "Under Construction" to skip a card and draw a new one — no pressure.
